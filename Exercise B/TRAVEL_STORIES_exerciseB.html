Eiffel Tower - Paris, France

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most iconic landmarks in the world, located in the heart of Paris. Built in 1889, it offers breathtaking panoramic views of the city.

Colosseum - Rome, Italy

The Colosseum is an ancient Roman amphitheater that once hosted gladiator battles and public spectacles. It is one of the most famous symbols of Ancient Rome.

Statue of Liberty - New York, USA

The Statue of Liberty is a symbol of freedom and democracy, located on Liberty Island in New York. It was a gift from France to the United States in 1886.
